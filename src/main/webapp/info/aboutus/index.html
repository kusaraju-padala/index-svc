About us

Juse is a first of its kind social networking application over news and affairs in India. It gives you flexibility to choose your interests and gives you the best news in that category without any bias. We only have one agenda that is to show you the best thoughts in the country. Our users can see and share their opinions with our community over the things that interests and matters to them.

We are trying to change the news and media industry, by creating our very own and very Indian news/social media application that offers

  • People a chance to express their “Respectful Opinions”
  • Publish “News that Matters”
  • First time ever in India a “Common Man Centric” news application.

We are going to be a fully digital organisation committed to our users needs and in bettering ourselves for an India we dream of. We are not going to follow the traditional methodologies of news and magazine industry. We meant to change the perception of people and create a platform where entire country will be able to express themselves and unite for a common goal.
