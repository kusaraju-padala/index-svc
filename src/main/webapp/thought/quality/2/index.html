Thought Rules

  1. A thought written in any language, should be targetting the news which it was written for.
  2. The thought should not be a small comment. It should explain in brief about your opinion on the news.
  3. Thought should have references to all the facts that you stated in the thought.
  4. We welcome respectable opinions only, if they are any abusive words or meant to spread fake news they won't be visible to user and will be moved to archives.
  5. We meant to change the perception of people and create a platform where entire country will be able to express themselves and better ourselves. Please cooperate with our policies in order to achieve a better country.
